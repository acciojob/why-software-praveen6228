"Why I want to become a developer?"

My curiosity for coding started already in 2018, when I signed up for a beginner programming course in Berlin (HTML & CSS), organized by “Tjejer kodar.” I started to slightly open the door to programming. I was immediately inspired by the women around me. They were all so creative, smart, and brave. However, due to my low self-esteem, I felt I was neither creative nor smart enough. Since I could not stop dreaming of becoming one of these women, I signed up for a course with “Tjejer kodar” held at the Swedish island Gotland. This time with an introduction to JavaScript. My curiosity increased, when I saw what actually can be built with code. However, my negative thought patterns still hindered me from taking the final step to change my career.
